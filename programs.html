Performing Team Program

Because we know is all about performance, the Performing Team Program provides with a full scope of solutions for professional football teams. This program enhance the HUB to its maximal potential optimising the resources and possibilities of the facility in favour to your team’s performance.

According to your demands, we adjust, shape and develop human and technical solutions to support your training plans inside and outside the football field, aiming to increase the possibilities for a high performance stage of your team.

All in a full privacy environment to only focus in your work.

Technical solutions

  • 3 football field with size and natural / hybrid grass approved by FIFA. Exclusive for your team.
  • Cameras in all football fields. Innovative video Tactical analysis in live time by Kognia.
  • Swimming Pool for recovery or Return to play process working without impact. Bike inside.
  • Football Gym with isoinertial devices, vibratory platforms and open & functional areas.
  • Lounge room for coaches to analyse pre and post training / match. TV screen & audio.
  • Conference & Meeting room for players and staff. TV screen & audio.
  • Cold water pool for lower limbs to improve recovery process.
  • Medical area with treatment benches and screening devices.
  • Recovery corner: Water, recovery drinks, fruit, nuts.

Human solutions

  • Physiotherapist to support your pre and post training treatment and RTP programs.
  • Strength and conditioning coach to support your pre, in, and post training programs.
  • Sport phycologist to develop your plans and targets during the season.
  • Nutrition counsel according to your daily work type and week load.
  • Video analyst to edit and present your trainings according to your Game Model.
  • Team coordinator.
  • *All technical and human solutions are presented according to your needs.

You decide the way

We work hand-to-hand with you to extend your ideas and optimise your plans according to your priorities, demands and targets.

Enhancing Program

Aiming to build knowledge with high standards for professionals of football, the Enhancing Future Program offers you the possibility of learning surrounded by a high performance football environment. We create educational programs with high degrees of interaction for students, where to experience the study, analysis and practice of the game from inside.

The hub produces theoretical and practical knowledge thanks to our professional network of specialists in the divers areas of football: Training methodology, sports medicine, leadership, technology applied in football, strength & conditioning, etc.

With the support of several universities around Europe specialised in exercise and sport science, we count with remarkable professionals to set high standards in our programs.

Technical solutions

Human solutions
