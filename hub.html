Our HUB

The HUB is an open and diaphanous space designed to provide in the same space different areas for staff and players for optimising your working time. Understanding the training as a set of several processes, the HUB is presented as a path covering the needs of your teams before during and after the training.

  • Meeting room for coaches to adjust the last details of the training. TV screen to project the training sheet, video or presentations.

  • Open area of treatment bench to prepare players for training. Physiotherapist support with massage, tape or manipulation for an optimal readiness.

  • Open area for pre-activation before training. Bikes, isoinertial devices and gym equipment to Increase blood circulation, mobility and muscle tone.
  • Cool down area to decrees progressively fiscal activity without impact. Bikes, mattress and fit-balls for flexibility.

  • Improve recovery with the effect of cool water. Pool for lower limbs.

  • Open area of treatment bench to recover players after training. Physiotherapist support with massage and manipulation for an optimal recovery. Game ready and pressure devices to support restitution.
  • Swimming pool for cold down after training, keeping blood circulation without impact.
  • Meeting room for coaches to visualise the training while players recover. The training is recorded and edited by Kognia, ready to be watched after the session.

Our team of professionals study and reorganise the HUB in relation to the needs of you team, customising the dynamics produced by the training process.
